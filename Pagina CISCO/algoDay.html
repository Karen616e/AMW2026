    Registration Procedure

    1. Select the appropriate payment method:
      • Paypal / Credit Card (participants outside Mexico)
      • National Bank Transfer
    2. Fill in the Registration Form and upload your proof of payment.
    3. Answer the short DEI Survey.

    You will receive a confirmation email sent by the organization committee.
    In case of problems or questions, contact us.

    Cancellation Policy

    In case of any cancellation or non-attendance, the registration fee are not refundable.
